WebGL

Purpose

The purpose of this step-up is to introduce you to WebGL and 3D graphics in the browser.

After this step-up you will recognise a few key concepts and have an idea on what to search for to learn 3D graphics programming.

Schedule

  1. History of OpenGL
  2. A tiny bit of math terminology, such as matrices
  3. A brief look at some super simple Three JS demos.
  4. Free hacking.
